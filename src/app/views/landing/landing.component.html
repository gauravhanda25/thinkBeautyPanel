About Us

Think Beauty is the online destination to discover and book beauty- related services and courses. The company was setup by two Bahraini entrepreneurs in 2017, taking advantage of the fragmented market within the Beauty industry

Think Beauty makes it possible to book makeup, hair, and nail services as easily as it is to book a hotel and order food. With Think Beauty, Your Beauty is our profession

Clients can discover new artists, book appointments online, and enjoy the services

Artists can show case their work, connect with new and existing clients and grow their business

Our aim is to become and remain the only mobile application you need when it comes to your beauty
